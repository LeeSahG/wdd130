Mild Thrill

This is a 3.5 km ride which has a mild dose of adrenaline. The rapids are just enough to give you a chance to enjoy the beautiful site and to have a thrilling adventure. The stop is by a shallow lake to view our live fish collection while enjoying warm beverages, waiting for a boat cruise to take us back to the "something", where you can enjoy a hot shower before you leave.

High and Low

If you are looking for a weekend trip out, book our 2 day 9 km ride. The first 3.8km are heavy rapids filled with adrenaline. The stop is at Mabhelani Resort for the evening, where you will be served with a 2-course meal. There are sharing rooms, singles and couple suites to choose from. The last 5.2 km is a mixure of mild and heavy rapids carrying you to our partner restuarant where you will wait for your boat cruise back, while you admire the view.

Site-seeing

This is our beginner-friendly 4km ride, which allows cruisers to enjoy the view. The mountain sides have a large variety of indegenous flaura for the cruisers to enjoy. Passengers might be lucky to spot a few local monkeys qunching their thirst by the lakeside or cooling off on a hot day.

15km Race

This is our monthly 15km race with few rapids for thrill. Group bookings are accepted of 6 individuals per boat to race. A prize of R2500 is up for grabs, and a spot on our wall of fame. The top 12 monthly winners get to travel to Zimbabwe in December for a 21 km race and a chance at winning R12000 and a 3-day all paid vacation at Victoria Falls. We operate at a first come first serve basis with complete passengers.
