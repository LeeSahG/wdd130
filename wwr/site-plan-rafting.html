Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this plan is to outline the different courses that will fit respective adrenaline preferences. An thrilling and beautifully unforgettable experience is offered, with alternative water activities offered for individuals who are not looking for any kind of adrenaline rush.

Audience

Our services are those of recreation and leisure. Water reafting is an outdoor recreation facility that is related to a river. We target a variety of customers based on what they want to benefit from the experience. We target people who want to go on a relaxing outing from work, students from institutions who offer outings, families who are on a trip to bond and have fun, and young couples looking for a thrilling date.
